Who are Snapchat Account Strategists?

Snapchat Account Strategists are official representatives of Snapchat who are authorised to communicate with advertisers via email, phone calls, and video meetings. Our role is to provide personalised support to help you optimise your campaigns, achieve your goals on Snapchat, and grow your business.

We work directly with Snapchat’s Sales teams, brand partners, and agencies to develop, execute, and improve creative ad campaigns. We are not third‑party consultants or external agencies — we are Snapchat employees.

2

How did you get my phone number?

Your phone number is associated with your Snapchat Ads account. When you created your business account, you provided contact information (email and phone number) for verification and billing. As an official Snapchat representative, we have access to that information exclusively to support your advertising activities.

We do not use your contact details for any purpose other than to help you improve your campaign performance. Your privacy is protected under Snapchat’s strict data policies.

3

How do I know you really work for Snapchat?

Email domain verification: All legitimate emails from Snapchat Account Strategists come from @snap.com or @c.snap.com domains. If you receive an email from one of these domains, it is safe to open, reply, and click on any links.

Phone calls: Strategists may call from numbers that appear local to your region. You can always ask for an email confirmation or request to schedule a video meeting through a verified Snapchat meeting link.

✅ Quick check

  • Check the sender’s email domain – it should end with @snap.com or @c.snap.com
  • Calendar invites come from snapchat.com domains
  • We never ask for your password or payment information over the phone

Why are you calling from a Doha number if you are not there?

Snapchat Account Strategists are based in various regions around the world, but our outbound calling system may display local numbers to increase pick‑up rates. If you receive a call from a Doha number (or any other international number), it is simply a technical routing feature to make it easier for you to recognise a local area code.

You can always:

  • Ask the caller to send you a calendar invitation from their official @snap.com email.
  • Request a video meeting to verify their identity.
  • Call back using the official Snapchat support channels if you prefer.

How do you have access to my account?

As a Snapchat Account Strategist, we have read‑only access to your advertising account performance data (e.g., campaign metrics, creative performance, pixel status). This access is granted internally by Snapchat to enable us to provide tailored recommendations. We cannot make changes to your account, view your billing details, or access your personal login credentials.

Any changes to your campaigns must be made by you (or your agency) within your own Ads Manager. Our role is purely advisory.

What happens if I book a meeting?

When you accept a meeting invitation from a Snapchat Account Strategist, you can expect:

  • A short (15–20 minute) video or phone call.
  • We will share a screen view of your account (with your permission) to walk you through specific opportunities.
  • You’ll receive a summary email after the meeting with clear next steps.
  • No sales pitch — our goal is simply to help you improve performance.

You are never obligated to take any action, and you can end the call at any time. If you prefer not to meet, you can simply reply “not interested” and we will respect your choice.
